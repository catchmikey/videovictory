FAQs

This sounds too good to be true! How do you do unlimited videos for a flat fee?

We’ve created a production process over several years that helps us speed up our workflow. And the types of videos we’re producing lean more on the less complex sides. These videos serve to amplify your existing content.

What types of videos can I get?

In general, we want to deliver videos that are punchy and fall within 60 seconds. Explainers, promo videos, video teasers, product demonstrations are all possible. If you can communicate or explain what the video should do and touch upon in an email, then we’re good to go.

What does unlimited really mean?

You can send in as many as requests as you want, seriously! We’ll work through your requests as fast as possible. You only pay one price no matter how much you use us. Say goodbye to project based billing and scope creep!

How fast will I get my videos?

Your producer begins working within 1 business day of receiving your request. Simple jobs get completed faster than complex ones. We work on one video at a time. Once all revisions are complete, a video is marked as done. We then move onto the next video and so on. Be sensible! Personalized videos aren’t mass produced on an assembly line.

I am interested. What happens after I sign up?

First you’ll go through our on-boarding process. This means we’ll schedule a call and make sure to get all your brand assets transferred to our team. Once that is complete you’ll be ready to use our service. We try to on-boarded new clients within 1-2 business days.

I’m want a video today. Can you help!

Our service is fast – but if you need help today, it’s probably best to find a DIY solution or pay a freelancer. We want to ensure consistent quality and so our process takes a few days to get rolling!
